Delicious Pasta Recipe

ingredients

  • pasta -400gm
  • tomato sauce -2 cups
  • grated cheese - 1 cups
  • non-stick pan - 1

instuctions

  • Boil pasta in salted water
  • Drain and mix with tomato sauce.
  • Sprinkle with cheese and serve.
  • make the pan hot before adding the pasta
